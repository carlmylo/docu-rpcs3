## NOTES

* Rock Band 1 guitars can be connected via USB but Guitars for Rock Band 2 (and later) **require an Xbox 360 Wireless Gaming Receiver for Windows to connect to your computer.**

![Xbox 360 Wireless Gaming Receiver for Windows](https://carlmylo.github.io/docu-rpcs3/images/btns/ctrls/360/receiver.png "Xbox 360 Wireless Gaming Receiver for Windows")

* RPCN Menus (for sending or accepting online invites) may cause a softlock. You may need an alternative input method to navigate these menus, such as [[a typing keyboard or a gamepad]](https://carlmylo.github.io/docu-rpcs3/ctrls_pads){:target="_blank"}.

## Mapping

Older guitars may develop problematic whammy bars which cause issues while mapping. **You may have to Filter Noise to map correctly.**

Effects switch (pickup selector) can only able be bound to the top half or the bottom half. The bottom half is suggested so you can disable it.

By default, XInput has most things bound correctly. You only need to remap the following:

| **RPCS3** | **XInput** | **Guitar** |
|:--------:|:-----------:|:-----------:|
| R1 | ![Right Stick](https://carlmylo.github.io/docu-rpcs3/images/btns/ctrls/360/rs.png "Right Stick") | ![Tilt](https://carlmylo.github.io/docu-rpcs3/images/btns/gtrs/ts.png "Tilt") | 
| L2 | ![Right Stick Click](https://carlmylo.github.io/docu-rpcs3/images/btns/ctrls/360/rsc.png "Right Stick Click") | ![Solo Buttons](https://carlmylo.github.io/docu-rpcs3/images/btns/gtrs/solo.png "Solo Buttons") | 
| Right Stick: <br> Down | ![Left Trigger](https://carlmylo.github.io/docu-rpcs3/images/btns/ctrls/360/lt.png "Left Trigger") | ![Effects Switch](https://carlmylo.github.io/docu-rpcs3/images/btns/gtrs/fx.png "Effects Switch") |
